About us

Car Maintenance Shop “Omar and Merjem" was founded on 24th April 2021. Our work began as a car maintenance shop for performing maintenance on cars.
However, as the business grew, we decided to start purchasing and selling tyres. In addition to selling tyres, we also perform tyre changing and balancing with the latest equipment.
We currently work on two locations, our HQ is in Municipality of Ilidža and the second location is in Nedžarići near King Fahd.

Services

We offer the following to our customers:

Changing tyres

Balancing of tyres

Sale of tyres

Maintenance check
